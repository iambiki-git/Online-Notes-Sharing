Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a note?

To upload a note, go to the 'My Notes' section and click the 'Upload' button. Follow the prompts to select your file and add relevant details.

Can I share my notes with others?

Yes, you can share your notes with others using the sharing link provided after uploading your note.

What types of files can I upload?

You can upload PDF files, as well as image files in JPG and PNG formats.

How do I delete a note?

To delete a note, navigate to the 'My Notes' section, find the note you want to delete, and click the 'Delete' button next to it.
